About Us

Fresh beans

When a raw, green coffee bean is picked, it contains the precursors to, but doesn't yet exude, those complex flavor profiles that give coffee its quintessential smell and taste. It's the roasting process that imparts a flavor explosion of chocolates, caramels, flowers, and fruits.

Great coffee

With Great Coffee comes great responsibility - That's why we blend only the finest beans to give our coffee its distinctive flavor and aroma. And because its fairly traded, it'll do great things for the places where it grows and the people who grow it.
